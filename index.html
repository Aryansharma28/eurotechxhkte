● Transitional care · Hong Kong pilot

Keep discharged elders well at home — so they don't come back.

CareBridge bridges the gap after a hospital discharge. A frictionless daily phone call to the elder, a reassuring app for their adult child, and one calm dashboard where a social worker keeps watch over five people — catching the small slips before they become a readmission.

How it fits together

Three roles, one care loop

Each role sees only what they need, at the fidelity they can handle. Information flows from the daily call into a shared picture that the family and social worker both act on.

The Elder

長者 · 受照顧者

Gets a warm daily check-in phone call. Answers a few simple questions in Cantonese. That's it — no app to learn.

The Adult Child

A simple phone app. Sees at a glance that mum is okay today, reads the plain-language summary, gets nudged only when it matters.
